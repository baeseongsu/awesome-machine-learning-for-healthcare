# Venues

A lightweight reference for conference and journal abbreviations used in this paper list. Links point to official venue, publisher, or society pages when available.

Proceedings publishers such as PMLR, ACM, Springer, and CVF are intentionally omitted from README venue labels unless they are part of the venue name.

## Conference List

### Machine Learning / AI

- **[NeurIPS](https://neurips.cc/)**: Conference on Neural Information Processing Systems
- **[ICML](https://icml.cc/)**: International Conference on Machine Learning
- **[ICLR](https://iclr.cc/)**: International Conference on Learning Representations
- **[AAAI](https://aaai.org/conference/aaai/)**: AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence
- **[IJCAI](https://www.ijcai.org/)**: International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence
- **[UAI](https://www.auai.org/)**: Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence
- **[AISTATS](https://aistats.org/)**: International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Statistics
- **[KDD](https://kdd.org/kdd2024/)**: ACM SIGKDD Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining
